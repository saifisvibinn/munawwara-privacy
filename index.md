# Privacy Policy for Munawwara Care

**Effective Date:** May 13, 2026

Munawwara Care ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your personal information is collected, used, and disclosed by Munawwara Care. This policy applies to the Munawwara Care mobile application (the "App") and its associated services.

By downloading, accessing, or using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

## 1. Information We Collect

To provide our services, especially emergency SOS routing and pilgrim coordination, we collect the following types of information:

### A. Personal Information
- **Account Data:** Your full name, phone number, email address, gender, and role (Pilgrim or Moderator).
- **Communication Data:** Information related to voice calls placed through the App, including carrier-based calls and Internet VoIP calls, such as call duration, timestamps, and connection logs.

### B. Device and Permission-Based Data
Google Play requires us to explicitly disclose the collection of sensitive device data:
- **Location Data (Foreground & Background):** We collect your precise device location using GPS.
  - *Pilgrims:* Location is collected in the background to ensure Moderators can locate you during an emergency (SOS) or to guide you back to your group.
  - *Moderators:* Location is used to broadcast Navigation Beacons to guide Pilgrims.
- **Microphone, Phone State, & Calling:** 
  - We require microphone access to facilitate WebRTC Internet VoIP calls.
  - We use the Phone permission to facilitate **carrier-based voice calls** between group members.
  - We read your phone state to integrate seamlessly with your device's native calling screens for both Internet and carrier calls.
- **Push Notifications:** We collect device tokens to send real-time SOS alerts, chat messages, and call rings.

### C. Usage Data
We collect diagnostic data, crash reports, and performance metrics to improve the App's stability.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following core functionalities:
- **Emergency SOS Response:** Your real-time location is shared strictly with authorized group Moderators when you trigger an SOS alert, allowing them to find and assist you.
- **Group Coordination:** Displaying Pilgrim locations on a secure, private map accessible only to your assigned group Moderators.
- **VoIP Communications:** Using your microphone and network connection to facilitate internet calls between Pilgrims and Moderators.
- **Account Management:** Authenticating your session and assigning you to the correct travel group.

## 3. How We Share Your Information

Your privacy is paramount. We **do not** sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We only share information under the following circumstances:
- **Within Your Authorized Group:** Your location, name, and SOS status are shared with the authorized Moderators of your travel group.
- **Service Providers:** We may employ third-party companies (such as Firebase for notifications or WebRTC for VoIP signaling) to facilitate our App. These third parties have access to your Personal Data only to perform these tasks on our behalf and are obligated not to disclose or use it for any other purpose.
- **Legal Requirements:** We may disclose your data if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.

## 4. Background Location & Google Play Policy

In compliance with Google Play Developer Policies:
- **Prominent Disclosure:** The App uses background location tracking.
- **Purpose:** Background location is strictly required to ensure the safety of Pilgrims. If a Pilgrim is lost, the App must be able to update their location to Moderators even when the App is closed or not in use.
- **Consent:** Users are presented with a clear, prominent disclosure requesting permission to use background location before the permission is requested by the OS.

## 5. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your personal data only for as long as is necessary for the purposes set out in this Privacy Policy. 
- **Right to Deletion:** You have the right to request the deletion of your personal data. You can request data deletion by contacting your group administrator or reaching out to us directly. 

## 6. Security of Your Data

We use industry-standard encryption (such as HTTPS/WSS for socket connections and WebRTC DTLS/SRTP for calls) to protect your data in transit. However, remember that no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

## 7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Effective Date" at the top.
